Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Vision & Integrity

The Southern Group of Companies is a Chennai and Kerala-headquartered conglomerate founded on the principle that bold diversification creates enduring value.

Since our founding in 2015, we have grown from a single real estate and construction enterprise into a multi-sector group with dedicated verticals in wind and solar energy, land transport logistics, and solar EPC. Every company in our portfolio is incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, governed with transparency, and operated to the highest professional standards.

The Macro Picture

Why Now Is the
Right Time

The Southern Group is positioned at the intersection of India's most dynamic growth sectors — renewable energy, logistics, and real estate.

250 GW

Non-Fossil Fuel Milestone

India crossed 250 GW of non-fossil fuel capacity in September 2025 — a 3× increase since 2014 and 5 years ahead of schedule. Solar alone reached 150 GW by March 2026. The remaining 250 GW target requires 50+ GW of annual additions through 2030. Tamil Nadu holds 22% of India's wind capacity.

Source: MNRE / PIB, 2025–26
₹29 L Cr

India Logistics Market 2025

India's freight and logistics market is valued at ₹29 lakh crore in 2025 and projected to reach ₹50 lakh crore by 2031 at a CAGR of 9.07%. Road freight commands 70% of the market. Tamil Nadu was ranked #1 in LEADS 2025 — South India generates 58% of India's freight tonnage.

Source: Mordor Intelligence / DPIIT LEADS, 2025
₹106 L Cr

India Real Estate by 2034

India's real estate market grows from ₹45 lakh crore in 2025 to ₹106 lakh crore by 2034 — a CAGR of 10.08%. Tamil Nadu recorded 11.19% GSDP growth in 2024–25, the highest in India in 14 years. Chennai posted a 24% surge in residential sales in Q2 2025.

Source: IMARC Group / Tamil Nadu GSDP Report, 2025
2070

Net-Zero Commitment

India's net-zero by 2070 pledge and its 50% renewable power target — already achieved in June 2025, five years ahead of schedule — create a sustained multi-decade policy tailwind. India now ranks 3rd globally in renewable energy installed capacity.

Source: COP26 NDC / MNRE, 2025

Our Home Ground

South India's
Economic Engine

South India punches far above its weight — generating nearly a third of national output from just 19% of land. Every subsidiary of the Southern Group is anchored in this high-velocity region.

31%

of India's GDP

South India contributes ~31% of national GDP from just 20% of the population — projected to reach 35% by 2030 as industrialisation accelerates.

₹31.19 L Cr

Tamil Nadu Economy

Tamil Nadu's GSDP stood at ₹31.19 lakh crore in 2024–25 — 11.19% growth, the highest rate in India in over 14 years. The state generates 8.93% of national GDP on just 4% of land.

₹4.62 L Cr

Tamil Nadu Exports FY25

Tamil Nadu exported ₹4,61,757 crore of goods in FY2025, driven by auto components, electronics, garments, and engineering. Chennai Port handles ~40% of India's container traffic.

₹13.11 L Cr

Kerala Economy

Kerala's GSP reached ₹13.11 lakh crore in 2024–25, buoyed by tourism, remittances, and infrastructure spend. The state has India's highest Human Development Index — a robust consumer base.

⚡
Energy Leadership Tamil Nadu ranks 2nd in wind energy capacity nationally (9,813 MW) and is a top-5 state for solar. The Southern Group's energy subsidiaries operate squarely in this high-output corridor.
🚚
Logistics Hub South India generates 58% of India's total freight tonnage. Tamil Nadu earned the #1 LEADS 2025 ranking nationally. Southern Transport is positioned at the core of this freight backbone.
🏗️
Real Estate Momentum Chennai recorded a 24% surge in residential sales in Q2 2025. Tamil Nadu posted 11.19% GSDP growth — the highest in India — fuelling demand for construction and commercial real estate.

One Group, One Ecosystem

How Our Companies
Work Together

The Southern Group is more than a portfolio — each company creates real, tangible value for the others, forming a self-reinforcing business ecosystem unique in South India.

SE → SSC

Build & Power

Southern Solar Co-op installs rooftop and ground-mounted solar PV systems on residential and commercial properties developed and constructed by Sarnith Enterprises across Tamil Nadu — creating a complete build-to-power capability within the Group.

SE → ST

Build & Move

Southern Transport provides dedicated logistics services to Sarnith Enterprises — moving construction materials, metals, and equipment across South India. This direct Group relationship ensures reliable supply chain performance for Sarnith's construction projects.

SSC → ST

Solar & Deliver

Southern Transport handles the last-mile logistics for Southern Solar Co-op's EPC projects — moving solar panels, inverters, and installation equipment to project sites across Tamil Nadu. This intra-Group arrangement reduces procurement costs and lead times.

SWP → SE

Audit & Optimise

Southern Wind Power conducts energy audits on Sarnith's commercial and residential properties — identifying efficiency improvements and recommending renewable energy integration strategies. The Group's wind and solar expertise directly enhances the green credentials of its built environment portfolio.
