Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Vision & Integrity

The Southern Group of Companies is a Chennai and Kerala-headquartered conglomerate founded on the principle that bold diversification creates enduring value.

Since our founding in 2015, we have grown into a multi-sector group with dedicated verticals in aluminum and iron scrap trading, iron and aluminum product trading, wind and solar energy generation, and pan-India land transport logistics. Every company in our portfolio is incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, governed with transparency, and operated to the highest professional standards.

The Macro Picture

Why Now Is the
Right Time

The Southern Group is positioned at the intersection of India's most dynamic growth sectors — scrap trading, renewable energy, logistics, and commodity trading.

250 GW

Non-Fossil Fuel Milestone

India crossed 250 GW of non-fossil fuel capacity in September 2025 — a 3× increase since 2014 and 5 years ahead of schedule. Solar alone reached 150 GW by March 2026. The remaining 250 GW target requires 50+ GW of annual additions through 2030. Tamil Nadu holds 22% of India's wind capacity.

Source: MNRE / PIB, 2025–26
₹29 L Cr

India Logistics Market 2025

India's freight and logistics market is valued at ₹29 lakh crore in 2025 and projected to reach ₹50 lakh crore by 2031 at a CAGR of 9.07%. Road freight commands 70% of the market. Tamil Nadu was ranked #1 in LEADS 2025 — South India generates 58% of India's freight tonnage.

Source: Mordor Intelligence / DPIIT LEADS, 2025
46.7 M T

India Scrap Market by FY30

India is projected to generate 46.7 million tonnes of scrap by FY2030, with ferrous scrap demand growing 43% and aluminum demand reaching 4.5 million tonnes. The government targets 50% scrap use in steel production — creating a massive, policy-backed procurement opportunity for organised scrap traders.

Source: Ministry of Steel / IMARC Group, 2025
2070

Net-Zero Commitment

India's net-zero by 2070 pledge and its 50% renewable power target — already achieved in June 2025, five years ahead of schedule — create a sustained multi-decade policy tailwind. India now ranks 3rd globally in renewable energy installed capacity.

Source: COP26 NDC / MNRE, 2025

Our Business Model

Three Pillars.
Five Companies.

Every company in the Southern Group operates within one of three interconnected verticals — metal trading, clean energy, and logistics. Together, they form a self-sustaining industrial ecosystem built to grow with India.

5

Active Companies

Five individually incorporated entities — each with its own directors, operations, and market — united under one founding vision and a shared commitment to excellence.

11+

Years in Business

Founded in 2015 with Sarnith Enterprises, the Group has grown steadily over a decade — adding new companies, entering new sectors, and building durable pan-India relationships.

34

Combined Services

Across scrap trading, product trading, wind energy, solar energy, and logistics, the Group collectively delivers 34 distinct services — covering procurement, supply, generation, installation, and distribution.

Pan-India

Market Reach

Headquartered in Tamil Nadu and Kerala, the Group's sourcing, supply, and logistics network spans every major industrial belt — from the northern plains to the eastern coastline and beyond.

Pillar 01 — Metal Trading

Sarnith Enterprises & Southern Enterprises

A vertically linked trading chain — Sarnith procures aluminum and iron scrap from suppliers across India, while Southern Enterprises sources finished iron and aluminum products directly from factories and distributes them to industrial buyers. Raw material to ready product, within the Group.
Pillar 02 — Clean Energy

Southern Wind Power & Southern Solar Power

Two dedicated energy companies — Southern Wind Power generates and supplies wind electricity from owned windmill assets, while Southern Solar Power generates solar power and sells panels, installation, maintenance, and repair services. Together, they cover India's two largest renewable energy streams.
Pillar 03 — Logistics

Southern Transport

The Group's logistics backbone — Southern Transport provides freight and cargo movement, public transport, last-mile delivery, dedicated fleet services, and multi-modal coordination pan-India. It also supports intra-Group logistics, moving scrap and products for Sarnith and Southern Enterprises.

Our Journey

Over a Decade of
Bold Ventures

2015

Sarnith Enterprises Founded

The Group is born. Sarnith Enterprises India Pvt Ltd is incorporated in Tamil Nadu on 27 March 2015, establishing our presence in aluminum and iron scrap trading across India.

2022

Entry into Wind Energy

Southern Wind Power India Pvt Ltd is incorporated on 26 May 2022 — the Group's first clean energy venture, focused on wind electricity generation, power supply, unit sales, windmill installation, and operations & maintenance.

2023

Logistics Expansion

Southern Transport India Pvt Ltd is incorporated on 16 July 2023 in Kerala, extending the Group's reach into land transport and freight logistics across South India.

2026

Solar Power Vertical

Southern Solar Power India Pvt Ltd is incorporated on 26 February 2026 — the newest pillar in our clean energy portfolio, delivering solar electricity generation, panel sales, installation, maintenance, repair, and consultation services pan-India.
