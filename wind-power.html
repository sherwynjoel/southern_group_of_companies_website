Company Overview

Clean Wind Energy for
a Sustainable India

Southern WP India Private Limited is a dynamic and forward-thinking renewable energy company operating under the Southern Group of Companies. We are in the business of harnessing the power of wind — one of nature's most abundant and sustainable energy sources — and converting it into clean, reliable electricity for businesses, industries, and communities across India.

We own and operate a growing network of windmill installations strategically located across multiple states in India. These windmills work continuously to generate clean electricity, which is then supplied to our clients — helping them reduce their dependence on conventional power sources, lower their energy costs, and contribute to a greener environment.

In addition to electricity generation and supply, Southern WP India Pvt. Ltd. also offers wind power unit sales — giving businesses and industries the opportunity to invest in their own wind energy infrastructure and generate clean power independently. Whether you are looking to purchase electricity generated by our windmills or invest in your own wind power unit, we have the expertise and resources to make it happen.

India is on a rapid journey toward clean and renewable energy, and Southern WP India Pvt. Ltd. is proud to be a significant contributor to that journey. With a passionate team of energy professionals, a robust operational infrastructure, and a deep commitment to sustainability, we are building a cleaner, greener, and more energy-independent India — one windmill at a time.

Market Context

India's Wind Energy
Opportunity

India is the world's fourth-largest wind energy market — and Tamil Nadu sits at the heart of it. With a national target of 500 GW renewable capacity by 2030, wind energy is at the centre of India's clean energy transition and a multi-decade policy tailwind is firmly in place.

47.6 GW

India Wind Installed Capacity

India's total wind installed capacity stands at 47.6 GW as of 2025 — the 4th largest in the world. Wind energy contributes approximately 10% of India's total power generation, with Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, and Rajasthan leading the national fleet.

Source: MNRE / Global Wind Energy Council, 2025
12 GW

Tamil Nadu Wind Capacity

Tamil Nadu holds 11,938 MW of installed wind capacity — second in India with a 22% national share. The state's capacity utilisation factor is expected to reach 29.6% in 2025–26, the highest since 2018–19, driven by new hybrid wind-solar installations.

Source: NITI Aayog / MNRE, 2025
14.97%

Wind Market CAGR 2026–31

India's wind energy market grows at 14.97% CAGR — rising from 53 GW in 2026 to 119 GW by 2031. New capacity additions, repowering of ageing turbines, and offshore wind development are the primary growth levers.

Source: Mordor Intelligence, 2025
500 GW

India RE Target by 2030

India has crossed 250 GW of non-fossil fuel capacity and must add another 250 GW by 2030 — requiring 50+ GW of annual additions. Wind is a core component of this target alongside solar, with Tamil Nadu earmarked for significant new wind capacity allocation.

Source: MNRE / PIB, 2025

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Wind Electricity Generation

At the heart of our operations is the generation of clean wind energy. Our windmill installations are strategically positioned across wind-rich regions of India to maximise energy output. These windmills operate continuously, generating significant quantities of clean electricity — backed by modern turbine technology, regular maintenance protocols, and experienced operations teams that ensure maximum uptime and consistent power output.

02

Wind Electricity Supply

The electricity generated by our windmill network is supplied directly to businesses, industries, and energy consumers across India. We offer reliable and competitively priced wind-generated electricity as a clean alternative to conventional grid power — designed to meet the energy needs of clients ranging from small businesses to large industrial units seeking a sustainable and cost-effective energy source.

03

Wind Power Unit Sales

For businesses and industries looking to invest in their own wind energy infrastructure, Southern WP India Pvt. Ltd. offers wind power unit sales. We provide high-quality wind power units — complete with turbines, towers, control systems, and all necessary components — enabling clients to generate their own clean electricity on-site. Our team assists through every stage, from selecting the right unit to installation and commissioning.

04

Windmill Installation & Commissioning

We provide end-to-end windmill installation services for clients who have purchased wind power units. Our experienced installation team handles site assessment, foundation work, turbine erection, electrical connections, and system commissioning — ensuring that every installation is completed safely, efficiently, and to the highest technical standards.

05

Windmill Operations & Maintenance

Keeping windmills running at peak performance requires regular and professional maintenance. Southern WP India Pvt. Ltd. offers comprehensive operations and maintenance services — covering routine inspections, preventive maintenance, component servicing, and emergency repairs. Our maintenance programmes are designed to maximise turbine uptime, extend equipment lifespan, and ensure consistent energy output.

06

Wind Energy Consultation

For businesses and industries exploring wind energy as a power solution, we offer expert consultation services. Our team assesses your energy requirements, evaluates site suitability for wind energy, and recommends the most appropriate solutions for your specific needs — guiding you through the technical, financial, and regulatory aspects of wind energy investment for a smooth and informed transition to clean power.
