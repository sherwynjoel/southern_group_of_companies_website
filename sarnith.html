Company Overview

The Foundation of
the Southern Group

Sarnith Enterprises India Private Limited is the founding company of the Southern Group, incorporated in Tamil Nadu on 27 March 2015. As the Group's cornerstone venture, it established our presence in three complementary sectors: real estate development, construction contracting, and scrap and metals trading.

The company delivers end-to-end capabilities from project inception through to construction completion and metals sourcing — forming a vertically integrated platform in the built environment and commodities space.

Market Context

India's Real Estate &
Construction Opportunity

India's real estate and construction sectors are among the fastest-growing in the world — driven by urbanisation, a structural housing deficit, and sustained government investment. Tamil Nadu is at the centre of this momentum.

₹106 L Cr

Real Estate Market by 2034

India's real estate market is valued at ₹45 lakh crore in 2025, projected to reach ₹106 lakh crore by 2034 — a CAGR of 10.08% driven by urban expansion and commercial demand.

Source: IMARC Group, 2025
₹92 L Cr

Construction Market by 2031

India's total construction market stands at ₹62 lakh crore in 2025, forecast to reach ₹92 lakh crore by 2031 at a 6.87% CAGR — one of the world's largest active construction pipelines.

Source: Mordor Intelligence, 2025

Metals & Scrap Trading

India's Growing Circular Economy

43%
Rise in India ferrous scrap usage, 2021–2024
46.7M
Tonnes domestic scrap generation projected FY30
50%
Target share of scrap in India's steel production by 2030

India's ferrous scrap usage surged 43% between 2021 and 2024 — from 24 million to 34 million tonnes. The government's push toward Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking targets raising scrap's share in steel production from 15% today to 50% by 2030. India is projected to import up to 30 million tonnes of scrap per year by 2030, making domestic sourcing and trading operations strategically vital.
