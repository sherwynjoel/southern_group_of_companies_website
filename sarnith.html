India's Most Reliable Scrap Trading Partner

Where Quality Meets
Reliability

Sarnith Enterprises India Private Limited is one of India's most trusted and established scrap trading companies, proudly operating under the Southern Group of Companies. Founded with a vision to streamline the scrap trading ecosystem in India, we have grown into a pan-India operation that connects verified scrap suppliers with leading manufacturing companies and industrial units across the country.

We specialise in the trading of aluminium scrap and iron scrap — two of the most critical raw materials in India's manufacturing and industrial sectors. Our deep understanding of the scrap market, combined with our extensive supplier network and logistics capabilities, allows us to deliver consistent, high-quality scrap materials to our clients — wherever they are in India.

At Sarnith Enterprises, we believe that scrap is not waste — it is a valuable resource. By facilitating the efficient movement of scrap materials from suppliers to companies, we contribute to India's circular economy, reduce industrial waste, and help manufacturers access affordable raw materials.

Market Context

India's Scrap Trading
Opportunity

India's scrap and secondary metals industry is undergoing a structural transformation — driven by government policy, sustainability mandates, and surging industrial demand. Sarnith Enterprises is positioned at the heart of this growth.

46.7M T

India Scrap Generation by FY30

India's domestic scrap generation is projected to reach 46.7 million tonnes by FY2030. With India targeting 300 MT steel capacity by 2030, demand for scrap as a primary raw material input will grow sharply — creating sustained, large-scale trading opportunities.

Source: Ministry of Steel / JPC, 2025
43%

Rise in Ferrous Scrap Usage

India's ferrous scrap consumption surged 43% between 2021 and 2024 — from 24 million to 34 million tonnes. The government's push toward Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking targets raising scrap's share in steel production from 15% today to 50% by 2030.

Source: World Steel Association / SIMA, 2024
4.5M T

India Aluminum Demand by 2026

India's aluminum demand is projected to reach 4.5 million tonnes by 2026, growing at a CAGR of 7.4% through 2030. Secondary aluminum — produced from scrap — is central to meeting this demand at lower cost and carbon footprint than primary production.

Source: Aluminum Association of India, 2025
50%

Scrap Target in India's Steel by 2030

India's National Steel Policy mandates that scrap account for 50% of total steel production by 2030. This policy-backed mandate creates a guaranteed, expanding market for organised scrap traders — making scrap trading one of India's most strategically important industrial verticals.

Source: National Steel Policy, MoS, 2025

Rise in aluminum scrap use in solar and EV sectors

Aluminium scrap is a critical raw material for automotive, aerospace, construction, packaging, and solar manufacturing industries. Secondary aluminium — produced from scrap — saves 95% of the energy required to produce primary aluminium, making it both economically and environmentally preferred. Sarnith Enterprises sources premium-grade aluminium scrap from verified suppliers and supplies it to manufacturers who require consistent, graded material.

Iron scrap is the backbone of India's steel and manufacturing industry. Sarnith Enterprises maintains a robust network of iron scrap suppliers across the country, enabling us to fulfil bulk orders efficiently and reliably for steel mills, foundries, and fabrication units of all scales.

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Aluminium Scrap Trading

We source and supply premium-quality aluminium scrap from verified suppliers across India. Our aluminium scrap is carefully assessed for grade and purity before supply — ensuring clients in automotive, aerospace, construction, and packaging receive material that meets their production standards.

02

Iron Scrap Trading

We maintain a robust network of iron scrap suppliers across the country, enabling us to fulfil bulk orders efficiently and reliably. From small manufacturing units to large industrial companies and steel mills, we cater to iron scrap requirements of all scales.

03

Supplier-to-Company Distribution

Our core strength lies in bridging the gap between scrap suppliers and industrial companies. We manage the entire process — from identifying and verifying suppliers to coordinating logistics and ensuring timely delivery — eliminating unnecessary middlemen and reducing costs.

04

Pan-India Scrap Procurement

With operations spanning the entire country, we are equipped to procure scrap materials from any part of India — whether the source is a metropolitan industrial hub or a remote manufacturing belt. Our team has the network and the expertise to handle procurement efficiently and at scale.

05

Bulk Scrap Supply

We handle high-volume scrap orders for large manufacturing plants, steel mills, and industrial units. Our bulk supply capabilities are backed by strong logistics partnerships, ensuring that large orders are delivered on schedule without compromising on quality.

06

Quality Assessment & Grading

Before any scrap material is dispatched, our team conducts thorough quality checks and grading. We assess scrap based on purity, grade, and composition — providing accurate and honest material details to our buyers so they receive exactly what they require.
