Company Overview

The Group's Logistics
Backbone

Southern Transport India Private Limited is the logistics arm of the Southern Group, incorporated in Kerala on 16 July 2023. Registered in Kerala and operating across South India, the company provides land transport services to commercial and industrial clients.

With three founding directors, Southern Transport brings a multi-stakeholder commitment to building reliable freight and mobility networks across South India.

Market Context

India's Logistics &
Transport Opportunity

India's freight and logistics sector is one of the fastest-growing in the world, propelled by e-commerce expansion, manufacturing growth, and massive infrastructure investment. South India — led by Tamil Nadu and Kerala — sits at the centre of this national momentum.

₹29 L Cr

India Logistics Market 2025

India's freight and logistics market is valued at ₹29 lakh crore in 2025 and is projected to reach ₹50 lakh crore by 2031 — growing at a CAGR of 9.07%, making it one of the world's fastest-expanding logistics markets.

Source: Mordor Intelligence, 2025
70%

Road Freight Market Share

Road transport commands 70% of India's total logistics market in 2025. The India road freight market alone is valued at ₹12.9 lakh crore and projected to reach ₹21.5 lakh crore by 2031 at an 8.84% CAGR.

Source: Mordor Intelligence / MRFR, 2025
#1

Tamil Nadu — LEADS 2025

Tamil Nadu was ranked the Exemplar coastal state in LEADS 2025 — the Government of India's Logistics Ease Across Different States index. South India generates 58% of India's total freight tonnage, anchored by Chennai, Kochi, and Coimbatore.

Source: DPIIT LEADS Report, 2025
40%

Chennai Port Container Share

Chennai Port handles 40% of India's total container traffic — making it the country's dominant eastern seaboard gateway. The new Vizhinjam deep-draft terminal in Kerala is set to further transform South India's maritime logistics landscape.

Source: ITLN / Port Research, 2025

Road freight remains the dominant mode — commanding 70% of India's logistics market — and its growth is accelerating. Policy & Regulatory Tailwinds

LEADS

Logistics Performance Index

LEADS 2025 ranked Tamil Nadu the Exemplar coastal state and Kerala a High Performer — recognition that reflects world-class road, port, and warehousing infrastructure directly benefiting freight operators in the region.

GatiShakti

National Master Plan

PM GatiShakti integrates road, rail, ports, and waterways into a single multimodal freight network. It directly reduces transit time and logistics costs for road freight operators through coordinated corridor development.

NLP

National Logistics Policy

India's National Logistics Policy targets reducing logistics costs from ~14% of GDP to under 8% — aligning with global benchmarks. This drives investment in tech, fleet efficiency, and integrated supply chain solutions across South India.
