Company Overview

Reliable Logistics and Transport
Solutions Across India

South Transport India Private Limited is a trusted and established logistics and transportation company operating proudly under the Southern Group of Companies. We are in the business of moving — moving goods, moving cargo, and moving people — across the length and breadth of India with the reliability, efficiency, and professionalism that our clients have come to depend on.

In today's fast-paced and highly competitive business environment, a dependable logistics and transport partner is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Delays in goods movement, unreliable freight services, and poor supply chain coordination can cost businesses significantly in time, money, and reputation. South Transport India Pvt. Ltd. was established to solve exactly these challenges — delivering logistics and transport solutions that businesses and communities across India can count on, without exception.

We operate a comprehensive and well-maintained fleet of transport vehicles supported by an experienced team of drivers, logistics coordinators, and operations professionals. Our network spans major industrial corridors, commercial hubs, and public transport routes across India — giving us the reach, capability, and flexibility to serve a diverse range of clients and transportation requirements.

From freight and cargo transportation to public transport operations and last-mile delivery services, South Transport India Pvt. Ltd. offers a complete portfolio of transport solutions designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes and sectors. We combine operational excellence with a deep commitment to safety, timeliness, and customer satisfaction — ensuring that every consignment we handle and every route we serve reflects the high standards of the Southern Group of Companies.

Market Context

India's Logistics &
Transport Opportunity

India's freight and logistics sector is one of the fastest-growing in the world, propelled by e-commerce expansion, manufacturing growth, and massive infrastructure investment. South India — led by Tamil Nadu and Kerala — sits at the centre of this national momentum.

₹29 L Cr

India Logistics Market 2025

India's freight and logistics market is valued at ₹29 lakh crore in 2025 and is projected to reach ₹50 lakh crore by 2031 — growing at a CAGR of 9.07%, making it one of the world's fastest-expanding logistics markets.

Source: Mordor Intelligence, 2025
70%

Road Freight Market Share

Road transport commands 70% of India's total logistics market in 2025. The India road freight market alone is valued at ₹12.9 lakh crore and projected to reach ₹21.5 lakh crore by 2031 at an 8.84% CAGR.

Source: Mordor Intelligence / MRFR, 2025
#1

Tamil Nadu — LEADS 2025

Tamil Nadu was ranked the Exemplar coastal state in LEADS 2025 — the Government of India's Logistics Ease Across Different States index. South India generates 58% of India's total freight tonnage, anchored by Chennai, Kochi, and Coimbatore.

Source: DPIIT LEADS Report, 2025
40%

Chennai Port Container Share

Chennai Port handles 40% of India's total container traffic — making it the country's dominant eastern seaboard gateway. The new Vizhinjam deep-draft terminal in Kerala is set to further transform South India's maritime logistics landscape.

Source: ITLN / Port Research, 2025

Policy & Regulatory Tailwinds

LEADS

Logistics Performance Index

LEADS 2025 ranked Tamil Nadu the Exemplar coastal state and Kerala a High Performer — recognition that reflects world-class road, port, and warehousing infrastructure directly benefiting freight operators in the region.

GatiShakti

National Master Plan

PM GatiShakti integrates road, rail, ports, and waterways into a single multimodal freight network — directly reducing transit time and logistics costs for road freight operators through coordinated corridor development.

NLP

National Logistics Policy

India's National Logistics Policy targets reducing logistics costs from ~14% of GDP to under 8% — aligning with global benchmarks. This drives investment in tech, fleet efficiency, and integrated supply chain solutions across South India.

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Freight & Cargo Transportation

Our flagship service is the safe and efficient transportation of freight and cargo across India. We handle a wide range of goods — from industrial raw materials and manufactured products to commercial merchandise and consumer goods — reliably from origin to destination across all major routes. Our freight transportation service is backed by a well-maintained fleet, experienced drivers, and a robust operations team that monitors every consignment from dispatch to delivery.

02

Logistics & Supply Chain Management

Beyond transportation, we offer comprehensive logistics and supply chain management services. We work with businesses to design and manage efficient supply chain operations — coordinating procurement logistics, warehouse management, distribution planning, and delivery scheduling to ensure that goods move through the supply chain with maximum speed and minimum cost.

03

Public Transport Services

South Transport India Pvt. Ltd. operates reliable public transport services connecting communities, towns, and cities across India. Our public transport operations are characterised by punctuality, safety, passenger comfort, and route efficiency — making us a trusted provider of public mobility solutions that improve the daily lives of the communities we serve.

04

Last-Mile Delivery Services

The last mile is often the most critical and challenging part of the logistics journey. We offer dedicated last-mile delivery services — ensuring that goods reach their final destination safely, accurately, and on time. Our last-mile delivery network is designed to handle the complexities of urban and semi-urban delivery environments, providing businesses with a reliable solution for completing their supply chain from warehouse to end customer.

05

Industrial & Commercial Goods Transport

We specialise in the transportation of industrial and commercial goods — including heavy machinery, industrial equipment, construction materials, bulk commodities, and packaged commercial products. Our fleet is equipped to handle diverse cargo types and sizes, with the right vehicles, loading equipment, and securing mechanisms to ensure that every consignment arrives in perfect condition.

06

Dedicated Fleet Services

For businesses with regular and high-volume transportation requirements, we offer dedicated fleet services — providing a committed fleet of vehicles exclusively for a client's operations. This service ensures consistent availability, priority scheduling, and seamless integration with the client's logistics operations — delivering maximum efficiency and operational continuity.

07

Multi-Modal Transport Coordination

We coordinate multi-modal transport solutions — combining road transport with other modes of logistics to create efficient, cost-effective end-to-end supply chain routes for our clients. Our team manages the coordination between different transport legs, ensuring smooth handovers and uninterrupted cargo movement from origin to final destination.

08

Express & Time-Critical Delivery

For shipments that require urgent or time-critical delivery, we offer express transport services with priority routing and dedicated vehicle allocation. Our express delivery team is trained to handle time-sensitive consignments with the speed, precision, and care they demand — ensuring that critical goods reach their destination within the required timeframe.
