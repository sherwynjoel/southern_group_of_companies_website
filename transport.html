Company Overview

The Group's Logistics
Backbone

Southern Transport India Private Limited is the logistics arm of the Southern Group, incorporated in Kerala on 16 July 2023. Registered in Kerala and operating across South India, the company provides land transport services to commercial and industrial clients.

With three founding directors, Southern Transport brings a multi-stakeholder commitment to building reliable freight and mobility networks across South India.

What We Do

Business
Activities

01

Freight Transport

Land-based freight movement services for commercial and industrial clients across South India, with flexible routing and scheduling capabilities.

02

Last-Mile Logistics

Dedicated last-mile delivery solutions connecting warehouses, distribution centres, and end clients across Kerala and Tamil Nadu.

03

Contract Logistics

Long-term logistics partnerships with manufacturing and retail clients, providing dedicated fleet and route management under service agreements.

04

Fleet Management

Management of owned and leased vehicle fleets including maintenance, compliance, driver management, and fuel efficiency optimization.
