Company Overview

Bridging Factories and
Suppliers Across India

Southern Enterprises is a leading iron and aluminum product trading company operating proudly under the Southern Group of Companies. We specialise in the trade and distribution of finished and profiled products manufactured from iron and aluminum — serving as the essential bridge between manufacturing factories and product suppliers across India.

In today's fast-moving industrial landscape, the seamless flow of products from factories to suppliers is critical to maintaining efficient supply chains and uninterrupted production cycles. Southern Enterprises was established with the precise purpose of fulfilling this need — ensuring that quality iron and aluminum products reach the right suppliers, at the right time, at the right price.

We have built a strong reputation in the industry for our reliability, product quality, and deep understanding of the iron and aluminum trading market. Our extensive network of manufacturing factories and supplier partners allows us to handle diverse product requirements with efficiency and professionalism. Whether you are a factory looking for dependable distribution or a supplier seeking consistent product supply, Southern Enterprises is the partner you can trust.

We operate across India, serving clients in multiple industrial sectors including construction, automotive, packaging, engineering, and infrastructure. Our experienced team manages every aspect of the trading process — from product sourcing and quality verification to logistics coordination and timely delivery — ensuring a smooth and transparent experience for every client.

Market Context

India's Iron & Aluminum
Product Market

India's steel and aluminum industries are among the fastest-growing globally — driven by infrastructure investment, manufacturing growth, and construction demand. Southern Enterprises operates at the commercial trading layer of this large and expanding market.

300M T

India Steel Capacity by 2030

India is targeting 300 million tonnes of steel production capacity by 2030 — up from 160 MT today. The National Steel Policy mandates domestic steel use in government infrastructure projects, directly expanding the market for iron product traders.

Source: Ministry of Steel, 2025
₹4.8 L Cr

India Aluminum Market by 2030

India's aluminum market is projected to reach ₹4.8 lakh crore by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 8.5%. Rising demand from construction, electrical infrastructure, and automotive manufacturing is driving consumption across all product categories.

Source: CRISIL / Aluminum Association of India, 2025
2nd

India's Global Steel Rank

India is the world's second-largest steel producer and is on track to become the largest by 2030. This positions domestic iron product traders as critical intermediaries in the largest industrial commodity ecosystem in Asia outside China.

Source: World Steel Association, 2025
₹185 L Cr

Infrastructure Investment Pipeline

The National Infrastructure Pipeline commits ₹185 lakh crore across 13,000+ projects — each project a consumer of iron and aluminum products at scale. Road, rail, port, and urban construction are the primary demand drivers for metals trading businesses.

Source: Invest India / NITI Aayog, 2025

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Iron Product Trading

We specialise in the trading of a wide range of iron-based products sourced directly from manufacturing factories. From structural iron products to fabricated iron components, we supply industries and suppliers with the materials they need to keep their operations running smoothly. Our iron product portfolio covers various grades, sizes, and specifications to meet the diverse requirements of our clients.

02

Aluminum Product Trading

Aluminum is one of the most versatile and widely used materials in modern industry. Southern Enterprises trades a comprehensive range of aluminum-based products — including profiles, sheets, sections, and fabricated components — sourced from trusted manufacturing units. We supply these products to suppliers and industries across India, ensuring consistent quality and timely availability.

03

Factory-to-Supplier Distribution

Our core competency is managing the efficient movement of products from manufacturing factories directly to suppliers. We handle the entire distribution process — product sourcing, quality checks, packaging coordination, logistics management, and final delivery — so that our clients can focus on their core business without worrying about supply chain disruptions.

04

Profiled Product Trading

We specialise in trading profiled iron and aluminum products — precision-manufactured components used across a wide range of industrial and construction applications. Our profiled product range includes extruded sections, rolled profiles, and custom-fabricated components sourced from leading factories.

05

Bulk Product Supply

Southern Enterprises is equipped to handle large-volume product orders for suppliers, distributors, and industrial clients. Our bulk supply capabilities are supported by strong factory relationships and efficient logistics networks, ensuring that high-volume orders are fulfilled without delay or compromise in quality.

06

Supply Chain Coordination

Beyond trading, we offer supply chain coordination services — helping factories and suppliers align their production and procurement schedules for maximum efficiency. We act as a reliable intermediary that keeps the supply chain moving smoothly, reducing delays and minimising gaps between production and supply.

07

Quality Verification & Product Grading

Every product that passes through Southern Enterprises undergoes thorough quality verification. We assess products based on material grade, dimensions, finish, and compliance with industry standards — ensuring that our clients always receive products that meet their exact specifications.

Within the Group

Group
Synergies

With Sarnith Enterprises

Sarnith Enterprises procures aluminum and iron scrap across India, feeding a direct supply chain to Southern Enterprises — which processes and distributes finished iron and aluminum products to industrial buyers. Together they form the Group's complete scrap-to-product trading loop, from raw material sourcing through finished goods distribution.

With Southern Solar Power

Aluminum mounting structures, frames, and extrusions are essential inputs for solar EPC projects. Southern Enterprises sources and supplies aluminum products to Southern Solar Power — reducing procurement lead times and cost for the Group's solar installations.
