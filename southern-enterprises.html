Company Overview

The Group's Metals
Trading Arm

Southern Enterprises India Private Limited is the Southern Group's dedicated iron and aluminum product trading company, operating pan-India. The company sources finished and semi-finished iron and aluminum products directly from manufacturers and factories, and distributes them to suppliers, dealers, and end-users across the country.

With a factory-to-supplier supply chain model, Southern Enterprises eliminates intermediaries — delivering competitive pricing, reliable inventory, and flexible delivery arrangements for clients in construction, fabrication, and manufacturing industries.

Market Context

India's Iron & Aluminum
Product Market

India's steel and aluminum industries are among the fastest-growing globally — driven by infrastructure investment, manufacturing growth, and construction demand. Southern Enterprises operates at the commercial trading layer of this large and expanding market.

179M T

India Steel Capacity by 2030

India is targeting 300 million tonnes of steel production capacity by 2030 — up from 160 MT today. The National Steel Policy mandates domestic steel use in government infrastructure projects, directly expanding the market for iron product traders.

Source: Ministry of Steel, 2025
₹4.8 L Cr

India Aluminum Market by 2030

India's aluminum market is projected to reach ₹4.8 lakh crore by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 8.5%. Rising demand from construction, electrical infrastructure, and automotive manufacturing is driving consumption across all product categories.

Source: CRISIL / Aluminum Association of India, 2025
2nd

India's Global Steel Rank

India is the world's second-largest steel producer and is on track to become the largest by 2030. This positions domestic iron product traders as critical intermediaries in the largest industrial commodity ecosystem in Asia outside China.

Source: World Steel Association, 2025
₹185 L Cr

Infrastructure Investment Pipeline

The National Infrastructure Pipeline commits ₹185 lakh crore across 13,000+ projects — each project a consumer of iron and aluminum products at scale. Road, rail, port, and urban construction are the primary demand drivers for metals trading businesses.

Source: Invest India / NITI Aayog, 2025

What We Do

Business
Activities

01

Iron Product Trading

Sourcing and supply of finished iron and steel products — including TMT bars, structural steel, MS angles, and pipes — directly from manufacturers to distributors, contractors, and dealers pan-India.

02

Aluminum Product Trading

Trade in aluminum sections, sheets, extrusions, and coils sourced from primary producers. Products supplied to fabricators, construction companies, solar EPC contractors, and industrial manufacturers.

03

Factory-to-Supplier Supply Chain

Direct procurement from manufacturing facilities to eliminate inefficiencies and deliver better pricing. Flexible lot sizes, reliable delivery schedules, and pan-India logistics coordination for clients across sectors.

Within the Group

Group
Synergies

With Sarnith Enterprises

Southern Enterprises supplies iron and aluminum products to Sarnith's construction projects — providing structural steel, TMT bars, and aluminum fixtures directly from factory sources. This intra-Group arrangement ensures material quality and price certainty for Sarnith's real estate developments.

With Southern Solar Power

Aluminum mounting structures, frames, and extrusions are essential inputs for solar EPC projects. Southern Enterprises sources and supplies aluminum products to Southern Solar Power — reducing procurement lead times and cost for the Group's solar installations.
