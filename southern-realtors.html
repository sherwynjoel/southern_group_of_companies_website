Where Land Becomes
Opportunity

Southern Realtors is the real estate vertical of the Southern Group of Companies — a dedicated property business built to serve buyers, investors, and landowners across Tamil Nadu and Kerala. We specialise in the sale of residential plots, commercial properties, agricultural land, and premium villa sites, backed by the trust and institutional strength of the Southern Group.

At Southern Realtors, we believe that buying land or property is one of the most significant decisions a person or business will ever make. Our role is to make that decision simple, transparent, and rewarding — by offering verified listings, honest guidance, full documentation support, and site visits that give every client the confidence to invest with clarity.

We operate across South India's fastest-growing property corridors — from Chennai's suburban expansion zones and Coimbatore's industrial and residential growth belts to Kerala's coastal and highland property markets. Whether you are a first-time plot buyer, a seasoned investor, or a business seeking commercial land, Southern Realtors is the partner that delivers.

Market Context

South India's Real Estate
Opportunity

India's real estate sector is one of the fastest growing in the world — and South India is at the centre of this transformation. Southern Realtors is positioned to serve buyers and investors as demand for land and property surges across Tamil Nadu and Kerala.

₹13T

India Real Estate Market by 2030

India's real estate sector is projected to reach ₹13 trillion by 2030, contributing 13% of GDP. Residential and commercial demand is accelerating across Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities — with South India capturing a disproportionate share of investment due to its stable governance, infrastructure push, and industrial growth.

Source: NAREDCO / Knight Frank India, 2025
18%

Growth in Tamil Nadu Property Registrations

Tamil Nadu recorded an 18% year-on-year increase in property registrations in 2024, driven by infrastructure projects, IT corridor expansion in Chennai, and growing industrial zones in Coimbatore, Hosur, and Tirupur. Residential plots and commercial sites are among the highest-demand categories.

Source: Tamil Nadu Registration Department, 2024
22%

Kerala Real Estate Appreciation Rate

Kerala's property market saw average appreciation of 22% in 2024, driven by Non-Resident Indian (NRI) investment, Kochi's smart city transformation, and growing demand for premium villas and gated community plots. Agricultural and plantation land in Kerala's highlands also commands strong and growing premiums.

Source: Kerala RERA / PropTiger Research, 2024
₹2.4L Cr

Infrastructure Investment Driving South India Land Demand

Over ₹2.4 lakh crore of infrastructure projects — highways, metro rail, industrial corridors, and logistics parks — are under development across Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Every major infrastructure announcement directly stimulates demand for adjacent land and commercial real estate, creating durable long-term value for landowners and investors.

Source: Ministry of Road Transport / NITI Aayog, 2025

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Land & Plot Sales

We sell residential plots, commercial land, agricultural land, and villa sites across Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Every listing is physically verified, title-checked, and encumbrance-confirmed before it is offered to a buyer — ensuring complete peace of mind with every transaction.

02

Residential Property Sales

Beyond raw land, we assist buyers in identifying and purchasing independent houses, villas, and builder-floor apartments across South India's key residential markets — including Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Kochi, and Thiruvananthapuram.

03

Commercial Property Sales

We source and sell commercial properties for businesses of all sizes — from small showroom sites along arterial roads to large-format industrial plots, warehouse land, and office building sales. Our commercial portfolio is spread across South India's most active business corridors.

04

Agricultural Land Trading

We specialise in the verified sale and purchase of agricultural land across Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Our team handles all patta, chitta, and adangal verification, ensuring buyers receive clear-title farmland with full legal standing and zero encumbrances.

05

Property Consultation & Advisory

Our experienced property advisors guide buyers through every decision — from selecting the right location and property type, to evaluating market value, understanding local regulations, and timing the purchase correctly. We make complex property decisions straightforward and well-informed.

06

Site Visits & Property Assessment

We organise and accompany buyers on physical site visits to every property we list. Our team provides an honest, on-ground assessment of the site's topography, access roads, neighbouring developments, water availability, and surrounding infrastructure — giving buyers a complete picture before they commit.

07

Documentation & Legal Assistance

From title deed verification and encumbrance certificate checks to sale agreement drafting, registration at the sub-registrar office, and post-purchase mutation — Southern Realtors assists clients through the complete documentation journey. We work with trusted legal partners to ensure every transaction is watertight.

08

Property Management

For investors and NRI clients who own property in South India but are not present to manage it, Southern Realtors offers property management services — covering periodic inspection, maintenance coordination, tenant management, revenue collection, and title update support.

Every property we list has been physically inspected and legally verified — title deeds checked, encumbrances confirmed, layout approvals validated. We do not list unverified properties. This saves buyers time, money, and the risk of legal complications after purchase.

Southern Group Backing

Southern Realtors operates under the institutional umbrella of the Southern Group of Companies — a conglomerate with a decade-long track record across scrap trading, clean energy, and logistics. Clients deal with a group that has an established reputation for compliance, transparency, and ethical business.

Deep Local Knowledge

Our team has granular knowledge of property markets across Tamil Nadu and Kerala — including micro-market pricing, development pipeline, local government norms, and upcoming infrastructure that will drive land value. We advise clients based on genuine on-ground insight, not just listed rates.

End-to-End Support

From the first property shortlist to the final registration stamp at the sub-registrar, Southern Realtors accompanies every client through the complete transaction. We are not a referral service — we are an end-to-end property partner who stays involved until the deal is fully closed.

Transparent Pricing

We provide honest market valuations and transparent fee structures. Clients always know exactly what they are paying for the property and what our service fees cover. There are no hidden charges, no last-minute add-ons, and no pressure tactics — only straightforward, ethical transactions.

NRI-Friendly Services

We have significant experience assisting Non-Resident Indians in purchasing, managing, and selling property in Tamil Nadu and Kerala. We handle documentation remotely, coordinate Power of Attorney arrangements, and provide regular updates — making the process seamless for clients based outside India.

Loan Liaison Support

We work alongside leading nationalised and private banks to facilitate home loans and land purchase loans for eligible buyers. Our team helps clients understand their eligibility, prepare documentation, and navigate the loan approval process — making property ownership accessible and financially structured.

Long-Term Investor Focus

We do not just sell a property — we help clients build a long-term real estate portfolio. Our advisory team tracks land appreciation trends, upcoming development zones, and infrastructure announcements to help investors enter the right markets at the right time.

South India's Key
Property Markets

Southern Realtors covers the most active and high-growth property markets across Tamil Nadu and Kerala — from metropolitan suburbs to emerging inland corridors and coastal sites.

CHENNAI

Chennai & Suburbs

OMR, ECR, GST Road, Tambaram, Perumbakkam, Kelambakkam, Oragadam, and Sriperumbudur — covering Chennai's vast suburban and IT corridor expansion zones, where residential and commercial plot demand remains consistently high.

KERALA

Kerala

Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, Thrissur, Kozhikode, and the highland districts — serving Kerala's premium residential and NRI property market, with expertise in coastal plots, plantation land, and gated villa site development in this high-appreciation market.

The Principles Behind
Every Transaction

At Southern Realtors, a transaction is never just a sale. It is a life-changing commitment our client is making — and we treat it with the seriousness it deserves.

T

Transparency

Complete honesty in every listing, every valuation, and every fee. Clients receive accurate information — not what they want to hear, but what they need to know to make a sound decision.

V

Verification

No listing goes live without title verification, encumbrance check, and physical site inspection. Our clients never inherit legal surprises because we do the work before the listing, not after the sale.

E

Expertise

Genuine, on-ground knowledge of South Indian property markets — pricing trends, micro-market dynamics, regulatory changes, and infrastructure developments — applied to every client advisory.

C

Care

Property purchase is one of the largest financial decisions in a person's life. We treat every client's investment with the same care and diligence we would apply to our own — because long-term trust matters more than short-term commissions.

I

Integrity

We will not recommend a property we would not buy ourselves. We will not misrepresent a price, a legal status, or a market condition. At Southern Realtors, integrity is non-negotiable at every step of every deal.
