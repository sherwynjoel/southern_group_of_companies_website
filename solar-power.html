Company Overview

Complete Solar Energy Solutions
for a Brighter India

Southern Solar Power India Private Limited is a comprehensive and trusted solar energy company operating proudly under the Southern Group of Companies. We are dedicated to making clean, affordable, and reliable solar energy accessible to homes, businesses, industries, and communities across India. As the country accelerates its transition toward renewable energy, Southern Solar Power stands at the forefront — delivering end-to-end solar solutions that empower our clients to embrace clean energy with confidence and ease.

Our operations span three core areas — solar electricity generation and supply, solar panel sales, and solar system servicing — making us one of the most complete solar energy solution providers in India. We do not just sell solar panels — we generate solar electricity, supply it to consumers, install solar systems, and provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure that every solar investment delivers maximum value over its lifetime.

India is blessed with abundant sunlight throughout the year, making it one of the most solar-rich countries in the world. At Southern Solar Power India Pvt. Ltd., we harness this natural wealth and convert it into clean, sustainable electricity — reducing carbon emissions, lowering energy costs, and contributing to a greener and more energy-independent India.

Our team comprises highly skilled solar energy professionals — from engineers and technicians to sales consultants and service experts — united by a shared passion for clean energy and a commitment to delivering exceptional service to every client. We believe that solar energy is not just a product — it is a long-term investment in a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable future.

Market Context

India's Solar Energy
Opportunity

India is the world's fastest-growing solar market — adding a record 44.6 GW in a single financial year and targeting 500 GW of clean energy by 2030. Tamil Nadu sits at the epicentre of this transition, with vast untapped rooftop potential and a thriving utility-scale pipeline.

150 GW

India's Solar Installed Base

India's cumulative installed solar capacity crossed 150 GW as of 31 March 2026 — including 110 GW utility-scale, 25.7 GW rooftop, and 14.1 GW KUSUM off-grid projects. India now ranks 3rd globally in solar capacity.

Source: MNRE, March 2026
44.6 GW

Record Annual Solar Addition

India added a record 44.6 GW of solar in FY 2025–26, exceeding its own 34 GW target by 31%. Every gigawatt installed requires professional installation and long-term maintenance — creating direct, sustained business for solar service companies.

Source: MNRE / PIB, 2026
19.05%

India Solar Market CAGR

India's solar energy market grows at 19.05% CAGR — from 145 GW in 2026 to 348 GW by 2031. The solar EPC sector grows at 10.1% CAGR, with ground-mounted solar projected at 12–16% CAGR through 2035, reaching 40–55 GW in annual installations.

Source: Mordor Intelligence / GM Insights, 2025
161%

Rooftop Solar YoY Growth

India's rooftop solar additions grew 161% year-on-year in the first nine months of 2025, driven by PM Surya Ghar. Tamil Nadu's rooftop penetration stands at just 1.55% — less than 2% of potential utilized — signalling massive headroom for solar companies.

Source: Mercom India / Blue Bright Energy, 2025

Policy & Regulatory Tailwinds

Surya Ghar

₹75,021 crore outlay targeting rooftop solar in 1 crore Indian households. As of December 2025, 23.9 lakh homes have already installed 7 GW — creating a sustained, subsidy-backed demand pipeline for solar panel sales and installation.

KUSUM

PM-KUSUM Agricultural Solar

PM-KUSUM targets 34.8 GW of agricultural solar with ₹34,422 crore in central financial support — covering solar pumps, feeder-level solarisation, and ground-mounted plants, all requiring solar panel supply and long-term service contracts.

India's 500 GW non-fossil capacity target by 2030 requires adding 50+ GW annually through 2030. Solar is the primary vehicle — and every installed megawatt creates a 25-year maintenance and service opportunity for companies like Southern Solar Power.

What We Do

Our
Services

01

Solar Electricity Generation

Southern Solar Power India Pvt. Ltd. owns and operates solar energy installations across India, generating clean and sustainable electricity from the power of the sun. Our solar generation infrastructure is built using high-efficiency solar technology and maintained by our expert operations team — ensuring maximum energy output, minimum downtime, and a reliable clean power supply.

02

Solar Electricity Supply

The solar electricity generated by our installations is supplied directly to residential, commercial, and industrial consumers across India. We offer reliable and competitively priced solar-generated electricity as a clean and cost-effective energy solution — catering to individual households, small businesses, large industrial facilities, and commercial complexes alike.

03

Solar Panel Sales

We supply and sell high-quality solar panels sourced from trusted and certified manufacturers. Our solar panel range covers a wide spectrum of capacities and specifications — suitable for residential rooftop installations, commercial solar projects, and industrial solar farms. Our sales team works closely with each client to recommend the most suitable solar panel solutions for their energy requirements and budget.

04

Solar Panel Installation

Our professional installation team provides end-to-end solar panel installation services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. We handle every aspect of the installation process — from site survey and system design to panel mounting, electrical wiring, inverter installation, and grid connectivity. Every installation is carried out with meticulous attention to safety, quality, and technical precision.

05

Solar System Maintenance

To keep a solar system performing at its peak, regular and professional maintenance is essential. We offer comprehensive solar system maintenance services — covering routine cleaning, performance monitoring, electrical inspections, inverter servicing, and component checks. Our preventive maintenance programmes identify and address potential issues before they affect system performance — maximising energy output and extending equipment lifespan.

06

Solar System Repair & Servicing

When solar systems experience technical issues or performance drops, our experienced service team is ready to respond. We provide prompt and professional repair and servicing for all types of solar installations — diagnosing problems accurately, sourcing genuine replacement components, and restoring systems to full performance as quickly as possible. Our service response is fast, reliable, and backed by deep technical expertise.

07

Solar Energy Consultation

For clients new to solar energy or exploring the best solutions for their specific needs, we offer expert consultation services. Our solar consultants assess your energy consumption patterns, evaluate your site's solar potential, and design a customised solution that meets your requirements and budget — guiding you through system selection, financial planning, installation timelines, and expected returns.
